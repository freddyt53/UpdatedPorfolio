Train Schedule

In this project, I created a train schedule application that incorporates Firebase to host arrival and departure data. Using Moment.js I was able to retrieve and manipulate this information. The website will provide up-to-date information about various trains that are inputed, namely their arrival times and how many minutes remain until they arrive at their station.
